NEED HELP?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Find answers to your questions about buying, financing,
and maintaining your vehicle with AutoZone Car Sales.

Do I need to have perfect credit to finance a car?
No! We work with various lenders to find financing options that suit your situation.
How long does the car buying process take?
Typically, it takes a few days from selection to paperwork, depending on financing and inspections.
Can I trade in my old car?
Yes! We offer fair trade-in evaluations to help reduce your new vehicle’s cost.
Do you provide warranties?
Many of our vehicles come with warranties or extended service plans for peace of mind.
Can I test drive before buying?
Absolutely! We encourage test drives to ensure you find the right fit.
What paperwork do I need to bring?
Bring a valid driver’s license, proof of insurance, and any financing documents if applicable.
